Corporate Tax Transparency Analysis

An analysis/mapping of ABN-holding corporate tax entities by Local Government Area (LGA), for Australian Financial Year 2021-22. Data sourced from the 2021-22 Corporate Tax Transparency Report published by the Australian Taxation Office (ATO)

Metrics measured include:

  • Total Income Earned
  • Total Taxable Income
  • Total Tax Paid
